#### Question 1:
**Q:** How far is Rose Villa from Jaffna Airport?  
**A:** We're approximately 12 km (15-20 minutes drive) from Jaffna International Airport (Palaly Airport). We offer convenient airport transfer services. Please arrange this at the time of booking by calling +94 77 123 4567 or emailing info@rosevilla.com.

#### Question 2:
**Q:** Is breakfast included in the room rate?  
**A:** Yes! Complimentary breakfast is included with all room bookings. We serve both traditional Sri Lankan breakfast (string hoppers, dosa, idli) and continental options from 7:00 AM to 10:00 AM.

#### Question 3:
**Q:** Do you have parking available?  
**A:** Yes, we offer complimentary on-site parking for all guests. Our secure parking area can accommodate cars, motorcycles, and bicycles, monitored 24/7.

#### Question 4:
**Q:** What are the nearby attractions?  
**A:** Rose Villa is ideally located near Jaffna's top attractions: Jaffna Fort (1 km), Nallur Kandaswamy Temple (2 km), Jaffna Public Library (1.5 km), and Jaffna Market (1 km). We can arrange guided heritage tours to all major sites.

#### Question 5:
**Q:** Can you host weddings at Rose Villa?  
**A:** Absolutely! We're an ideal venue for intimate heritage weddings, accommodating up to 80 guests. We offer full catering, decoration, and event coordination services. Contact us at info@rosevilla.com for wedding packages.

#### Question 6:
**Q:** Is Wi-Fi available and free?  
**A:** Yes, high-speed Wi-Fi is complimentary throughout the entire property, including all guest rooms, common areas, and gardens. The network is secure and suitable for video calls and streaming.

#### Question 7:
**Q:** What makes Rose Villa different from other hotels?  
**A:** Rose Villa is Jaffna's only authentic colonial heritage hotel from the 1800s. We accommodate a maximum of 12 guests, ensuring personalized service. Our unique offerings include curated cultural experiences, traditional Tamil cuisine, and an exclusive heritage atmosphere.

#### Question 8:
**Q:** Do you offer airport transfers?  
**A:** Yes, we provide airport transfer services to/from Jaffna International Airport and can arrange transportation throughout Northern Sri Lanka. Please book at least 24 hours in advance.

#### Question 9:
**Q:** Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?  
**A:** Absolutely! We cater to all dietary requirements including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, halal, and specific allergies. Please inform us at booking or at least 24 hours in advance.

#### Question 10:
**Q:** What is the cancellation policy?  
**A:** We offer flexible cancellation: Free cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in for a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours incur a one-night charge. Please review our full policy at rosevilla.com/faq.
